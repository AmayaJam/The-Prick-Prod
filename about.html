Artist Statement

From the beginning, I knew I wanted to make some kind of satirical site.

In a world where information overload and digital noise overwhelm our senses, my parody site offers a tongue-in-cheek escape into a realm where absurdity reigns supreme. By embracing the absurdity of censorship in the pursuit of 'proper' writing, I invite viewers to reconsider their preconceived notions and explore the nuances of language and expression.

Ultimately, my goal is to spark conversation and critical thinking about the ways in which language shapes our perceptions and interactions. Through humor and satire, I hope to inspire a deeper understanding of the complexities of censorship and the importance of linguistic freedom in our ever-changing society.

(I'm making fun of myself more than anything since I have zero scruples when it comes to correcting someone's grammar over text even if I understand them perfectly)
